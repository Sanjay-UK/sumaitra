ABOUT THE COMPANY

A Nanotechnology-Driven Startup

Sumaitra Innovations Private Limited is engaged in advanced research, development, and production of high-performance nanomaterials and electronic functional materials.

With a strong scientific foundation and incubation support from Mount Carmel College, Bengaluru, the company bridges the gap between academic research and industrial deployment of next-generation nanomaterials.

Vision

To achieve global recognition in nanotechnology-driven innovation by developing transformative nanomaterials, smart sensors, and intelligent solutions that advance sustainability, industry, and human well-being.

Mission

To innovate and manufacture advanced nanomaterials, smart sensors, and electronic components through cutting-edge R&D; for high-quality, reliable, and sustainable next-generation solutions.
